About Me

I am a senior computer science student at Western Governors University. Currently, I work at C3i Solutions, an HCL company, as a Hardware Technician. I also was on assignment with HCL as a VR Developer for Metaverse research. Before that, I worked at Prudential as a Data Entry Operator where I entered customer account data from source documents into various systems. I previously attended the University of Scranton before the pandemic and worked there as a technical consultant where I provided support for faculty as well as students. Additionally, I created and edited faculty web pages, gave presentations on specific technologies, and instructed others in using the university's learning management system, Desire2Learn(D2L).
